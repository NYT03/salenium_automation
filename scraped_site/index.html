About Us

Vision

Urban Snap envisions vibrant, thriving cities where citizens actively participate in addressing urban challenges. Through our innovative platform, we aspire to foster a culture of civic engagement, where individuals can easily report issues and collaborate with local authorities to create cleaner, safer, and more efficient urban environments.

Mission

At Urban Snap, our mission is to revolutionize urban problem-solving in India by providing a seamless digital platform for citizens to report and monitor issues in their communities. By empowering individuals to capture and upload images of problems directly to Municipal Corporations, we strive to expedite the resolution process, ultimately contributing to the development of sustainable and inclusive cities for all.

Products

Urban Snap: "Empowering Change Makers"

Urban Snap App represents a paradigm shift in urban management through its pioneering use of deep learning and geolocation tagging technologies. These cutting-edge tools not only streamline the process of reporting urban issues but also enable predictive analytics for proactive problem-solving.

By facilitating faster response times and promoting community engagement, Urban Snap App stands as a beacon of efficiency and innovation in addressing urban challenges within Naya & Vikasit Bharat.

Features

Deep Learning Models

Urban Snap App harnesses the power of advanced deep learning technology to streamline issue reporting in urban environments. Through sophisticated algorithms, the app can quickly analyze and categorize incoming reports, enabling faster response times and more efficient allocation of resources. This cutting-edge approach ensures that problems are identified and addressed promptly, leading to a smoother functioning of urban infrastructure.

Data Analytics

Leveraging geolocation tagging and image detection, Urban Snap App not only facilitates rapid issue reporting but also empowers predictive analytics. By analyzing patterns and trends in reported issues, the app can anticipate potential problems before they escalate, allowing authorities to take proactive measures. This data-driven approach enables more strategic decision-making and resource allocation, ultimately contributing to the overall improvement of urban living standards.

Digital Community

Urban Snap App extends beyond its reporting functionality to foster an interactive community dedicated to urban solutions. Users engage, share insights, and collaborate to address common issues, promoting collective problem-solving and fostering civic responsibility. By bridging the gap between residents and authorities, the app facilitates the co-creation of tailored solutions that meet the specific needs of the local community.

Data Analytics

From Data to Decisions

The Urban Snap app will use photos of municipal complaints, such as potholes and sewage issues, to perform data analytics. By categorizing and mapping these issues with image recognition and geotagging, the app will highlight problem areas and identify patterns. This will help city officials prioritize resources and improve urban infrastructure, ultimately enhancing community satisfaction.
